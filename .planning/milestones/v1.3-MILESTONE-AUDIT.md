### Integration Note: PASS-04 Workflow Passthrough

Workflow steps in `slide-assembly.ts` and `drive-folders.ts` call `getDriveClient()`/`getSlidesClient()` without `GoogleAuthOptions`, always using service account. The `fetchWithGoogleAuth` headers sent for workflow starts are received but not forwarded to workflow step context. This was explicitly deferred during Phase 23 planning.

**Impact:** Low. Service account fallback is the designed behavior per GAPI-03. All interactive route handlers (template create, staleness check, thumbnails, ingestion trigger) correctly use user-delegated tokens.
